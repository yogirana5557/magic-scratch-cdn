Privacy Policy for Magic Scratch Surprise

Last updated: February 2025

Magic Scratch Surprise (“the App”) is developed by an individual developer, Yadnyesh Rana. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, phone number, or any personal details.

2. Information We May Collect Automatically

The App may collect non-personal, anonymous data for improving performance and user experience:

  • Crash data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Basic device info such as OS version, manufacturer
  • Anonymous usage stats (via Firebase limited analytics)

No data collected can identify you personally.

3. Firebase Firestore Usage

The App uses Firebase Firestore only to load scratch card content (images, titles, facts). No user-generated content or personal data is uploaded.

4. How We Use the Information

  • Improve stability & performance
  • Fix crashes
  • Enhance user experience

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Crashlytics)
  • Netlify (to host images/content)

Their privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Netlify Privacy Policy

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for all ages and does not collect personal data. However, parental guidance is recommended for young children.

7. Data Security

We do not store personal data, but Firebase ensures secure storage of app content.
